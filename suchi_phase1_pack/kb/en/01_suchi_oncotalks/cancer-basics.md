## What cancer is

Cancer is uncontrolled growth of abnormal cells. Sometimes it forms a lump (tumour) and in some cases can spread to other parts of the body (metastasis).

## Key points

- Cancer is a disease where cells grow and divide in an uncontrolled way
- Not all tumours are cancerous - some are benign (non-cancerous)
- Early detection and treatment improve outcomes
- Many types of cancer are treatable, especially when caught early
