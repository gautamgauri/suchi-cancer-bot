# Coping with Cancer

You may have just learned that you have cancer. Or you may be in treatment, finishing treatment, or have a friend or family member with cancer. Having cancer changes your life and the lives of those around you. The symptoms and [side effects](/about-cancer/treatment/side-effects) of the disease and its treatment may cause certain physical changes, but they can also affect the way you feel and how you live.

The information in this section is meant to help you learn how to cope with cancer and the many issues and concerns that occur when you have the disease. There are resources available to help you learn about your disease and about the people who can help you.
