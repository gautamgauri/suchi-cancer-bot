Forming Connections to Address Rare Brain Tumor Treatment and Care

NCI-CONNECT focuses on caring for people with rare brain and spine tumors. The CONNECT team uses a comprehensive, multidisciplinary approach, doing basic science research, clinical trials, and community outreach to improve treatments for those diagnosed with these cancers.
