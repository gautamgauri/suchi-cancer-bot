# The Role of Immunotherapy in Childhood Cancer Treatment Facebook Live

On October 25, 2018, NCI hosted a Facebook Live event on the role of immunotherapy in childhood cancers such as leukemia. [Dr. Nirali Shah](https://ccr.cancer.gov/Pediatric-Oncology-Branch/nirali-n-shah "Follow link") from NCI was joined by [Carlos Sandi](https://www.stbaldricks.org/blog/post/when-research-offers-hope-parents-continue-to-advance-the-mission), St. Baldrick’s Foundation advocate and childhood cancer parent. Together they discussed how research and advocacy play a role in advancing treatment for childhood cancer.
