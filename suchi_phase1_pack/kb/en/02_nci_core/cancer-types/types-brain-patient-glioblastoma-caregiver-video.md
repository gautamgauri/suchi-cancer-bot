Glioblastoma Caregiver Journey

Barbara shares her experience caring for her husband, Bob, who had an aggressive type of brain cancer called glioblastoma. She describes their challenges of finding an accurate diagnosis and dealing with treatment and its side effects. She also shares ways to cope that she and Bob found helpful.
