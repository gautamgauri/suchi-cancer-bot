## Use in Cancer

Trabectedin
is approved to treat:

- **[liposarcoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46028&version=Patient&language=English)** or **[leiomyosarcoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46027&version=Patient&language=English)** (types of [soft tissue sarcoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46584&version=Patient&language=English)) that cannot be removed by [surgery](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45570&version=Patient&language=English) or has spread to other parts of the body. It is used in patients who have already been treated with [anthracycline](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44916&version=Patient&language=English) [chemotherapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45214&version=Patient&language=English)

Trabectedin
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
cancer.
