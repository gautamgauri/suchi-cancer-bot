## Use in Cancer

Etoposide phosphate is approved to be used with other [drugs](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=348921&version=Patient&language=English) to treat:

- **[Small cell lung cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45327&version=Patient&language=English).** It is used with [cisplatin](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45230&version=Patient&language=English) as [first-line therapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=346494&version=Patient&language=English).
- **[Testicular cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=445090&version=Patient&language=English).** It is used in patients who have already been treated with [surgery](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45570&version=Patient&language=English), [radiation therapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44971&version=Patient&language=English), or other [chemotherapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45214&version=Patient&language=English) and have not gotten better.

Etoposide phosphate is also available in a different form called [etoposide](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45189&version=Patient&language=English). For more information, see the Drug Information Summary for [Etoposide](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/etoposide).

Etoposide phosphate is also being studied in the treatment of other types of [cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
