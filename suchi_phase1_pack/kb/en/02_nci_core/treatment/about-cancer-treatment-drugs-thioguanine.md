## Use in Cancer

Thioguanine
is approved
to be used as [remission induction therapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46560&version=Patient&language=English) and remission [consolidation therapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45654&version=Patient&language=English) to treat:

- **[Acute myeloid leukemia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46347&version=Patient&language=English)** (AML).

Thioguanine
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
[cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
