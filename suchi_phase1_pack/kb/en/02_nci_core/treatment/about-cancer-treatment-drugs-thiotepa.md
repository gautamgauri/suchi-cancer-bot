## Use in Cancer

Thiotepa
is approved
to treat:

- **[Bladder cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=444968&version=Patient&language=English).**
- **[Breast cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=444971&version=Patient&language=English).**
- **[Malignant pleural effusion](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=524207&version=Patient&language=English),** **[malignant pericardial effusion](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=765476&version=Patient&language=English),** and **[malignant peritoneal effusion](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=765478&version=Patient&language=English).**
- **[Ovarian cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=445074&version=Patient&language=English).**

Thiotepa
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
cancer and as part of a [regimen](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45864&version=Patient&language=English) to prepare patients for [bone marrow](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45263&version=Patient&language=English) and [stem cell transplants](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46695&version=Patient&language=English).
