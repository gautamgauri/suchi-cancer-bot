## Use in Cancer

Uridine triacetate
is approved to be used in the emergency treatment of:

- **[Cardiac](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45634&version=Patient&language=English) (heart) or [central nervous system](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46481&version=Patient&language=English) [toxicity](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=445093&version=Patient&language=English) or other serious [side effects](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46580&version=Patient&language=English)** within 4 days of ending treatment with [fluorouracil](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46090&version=Patient&language=English) or [capecitabine](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45565&version=Patient&language=English).
- **[Overdoses](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=450114&version=Patient&language=English)** of fluorouracil or capecitabine.

Uridine triacetate is also being studied in the treatment of other [conditions](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=651193&version=Patient&language=English).
