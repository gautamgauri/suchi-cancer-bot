## Use in Cancer

Lomustine
is approved
to be used alone or with other [drugs](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=348921&version=Patient&language=English) to treat:

- **[Brain tumors](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=387264&version=Patient&language=English).** It is used in patients who have already been treated with [surgery](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45570&version=Patient&language=English) or [radiation therapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44971&version=Patient&language=English).
- **[Hodgkin lymphoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=270800&version=Patient&language=English).** It is used with [chemotherapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45214&version=Patient&language=English) in patients whose disease got worse after other types of treatment.

Lomustine
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
[cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
