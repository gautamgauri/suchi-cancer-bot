## Use in Cancer

Ceritinib
is approved
to treat:

- **[Non-small cell lung cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45323&version=Patient&language=English)** that is [ALK positive](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=796880&version=Patient&language=English) and has [metastasized](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46283&version=Patient&language=English) (spread to other parts of the body). It is used in adults.

Ceritinib
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
cancer.
