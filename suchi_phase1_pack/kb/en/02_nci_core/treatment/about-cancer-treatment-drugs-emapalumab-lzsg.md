## Use in Cancer

Emapalumab-lzsg
is approved to treat:

- **[Hemophagocytic lymphohistiocytosis](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=488415&version=Patient&language=English)** (HLH) that is [inherited](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45098&version=Patient&language=English). It is used in adults and children (newborn and older) whose disease is [refractory](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=350245&version=Patient&language=English) (does not [respond](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44085&version=Patient&language=English) to treatment), has [recurred](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46556&version=Patient&language=English), has gotten worse, or cannot be treated with other therapies for HLH.

Emapalumab-lzsg
is also being studied in the treatment of other conditions.
