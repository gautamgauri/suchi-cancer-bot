## Use in Cancer

Amifostine
is approved to prevent:

- **Renal [(kidney)](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46325&version=Patient&language=English) [toxicity](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=445093&version=Patient&language=English)**. It is used to reduce the [nephrotoxic](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44533&version=Patient&language=English) effects caused by repeated treatment with [cisplatin](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45230&version=Patient&language=English) for [advanced](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=478743&version=Patient&language=English) [ovarian cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=445074&version=Patient&language=English).
- **[Xerostomia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44537&version=Patient&language=English)** (dry mouth). It is used to decrease dryness in the mouth caused by [radiation therapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44971&version=Patient&language=English) after [surgery](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45570&version=Patient&language=English) in some patients with [head and neck cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=257519&version=Patient&language=English).

Amifostine
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
[cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
