## Use in Cancer

Radium 223 dichloride
is approved
to treat:

- **[Prostate cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=445079&version=Patient&language=English)** that is [castrate resistant](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=753525&version=Patient&language=English) (has not responded to treatments that lower [testosterone](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45581&version=Patient&language=English) levels). It is used in patients whose [cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English) has [metastasized](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46283&version=Patient&language=English) (spread) to the bones and is causing [symptoms](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45022&version=Patient&language=English) but has not spread to other [organs](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=257523&version=Patient&language=English).

Radium 223 dichloride
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
cancer.
