## Use in Cancer

Melphalan
is approved for [palliative treatment](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45815&version=Patient&language=English) of:

- **[Multiple myeloma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45793&version=Patient&language=English).**
- **[Ovarian epithelial cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46768&version=Patient&language=English)** that cannot be removed by [surgery](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45570&version=Patient&language=English).

Melphalan
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
[cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).

Melphalan is also available in an [injectable](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44678&version=Patient&language=English) form for patients who cannot take melphalan by mouth. For more information, see the Drug Information Summary for [Melphalan Hydrochloride](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/melphalan-hydrochloride).
