## Use in Cancer

Asparaginase erwinia chrysanthemi
is approved
to be used with other [drugs](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=348921&version=Patient&language=English) to treat:

- **[Acute lymphoblastic leukemia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45586&version=Patient&language=English).** It is a form of [asparaginase](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45339&version=Patient&language=English) made from the [bacterium](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44123&version=Patient&language=English) *Erwinia chrysanthemi*. It is used in patients who can no longer take asparaginase made from *E. coli*.

Asparaginase erwinia chrysanthemi
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
[cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).

This drug is also available in a [recombinant](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44619&version=Patient&language=English) form. For more information, see the Drug Information Summary for [Asparaginase Erwinia Chrysanthemi (Recombinant)-rywn](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/asparaginase-erwinia-chrysanthemi-recombinant).
