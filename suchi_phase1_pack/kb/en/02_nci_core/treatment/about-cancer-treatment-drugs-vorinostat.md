## Use in Cancer

Vorinostat is approved to treat:

- **[Cutaneous T-cell lymphoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46771&version=Patient&language=English)** that has not gotten better, has gotten worse, or has [recurred](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45862&version=Patient&language=English) (come back) during or after two [systemic therapies](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45922&version=Patient&language=English).

Vorinostat is also being studied in the treatment of other types of [cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
