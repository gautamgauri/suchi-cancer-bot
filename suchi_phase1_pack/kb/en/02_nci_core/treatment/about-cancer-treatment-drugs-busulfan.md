## Use in Cancer

Busulfan
is approved
to treat:

- **[Chronic myelogenous leukemia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44901&version=Patient&language=English)** (CML).
- It is used as [palliative treatment](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45815&version=Patient&language=English). This use is approved for the Myleran brand of busulfan.
- It is also used with other [drugs](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=348921&version=Patient&language=English) to prepare patients with CML for a [stem cell transplant](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46695&version=Patient&language=English). This use is approved for the Busulfex brand of busulfan.

Busulfan
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
[cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
