## Use in Cancer

Gemtuzumab ozogamicin is approved to treat:

- [Acute myeloid leukemia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46347&version=Patient&language=English) that is [CD33 positive](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=798790&version=Patient&language=English).
- It is used in adults and children aged 1 month and older with newly-[diagnosed](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46450&version=Patient&language=English) disease.
- It is used in adults and children aged 2 years and older whose disease has [relapsed](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45866&version=Patient&language=English) or is [refractory](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45863&version=Patient&language=English) (does not [respond](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44085&version=Patient&language=English) to treatment).

Gemtuzumab ozogamicin is also being studied in the treatment of other types of [cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
