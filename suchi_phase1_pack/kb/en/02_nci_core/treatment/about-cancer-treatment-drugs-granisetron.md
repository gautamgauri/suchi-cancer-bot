## Use in Cancer

Granisetron
is approved to be used alone or with other [drugs](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=348921&version=Patient&language=English) to prevent:

- **[Nausea](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=390302&version=Patient&language=English) and [vomiting](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=390324&version=Patient&language=English)** caused by [chemotherapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45214&version=Patient&language=English). It is used in adults.

Granisetron
is also available in a different form called [granisetron hydrochloride](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=616128&version=Patient&language=English). For more information, see the Drug Information Summary for [Granisetron Hydrochloride](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/granisetronhydrochloride).
