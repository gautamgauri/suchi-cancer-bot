## Use in Cancer

Darolutamide
is approved to treat:

- **[Prostate cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=445079&version=Patient&language=English).** It is used in adults whose cancer:
- Has spread to other parts of the body and responds to treatment that lowers [testosterone](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45581&version=Patient&language=English) levels. It is used with [docetaxel](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45382&version=Patient&language=English).
- Has not spread to other parts of the body and no longer responds to treatment that lowers testosterone levels.

Darolutamide
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
[cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
