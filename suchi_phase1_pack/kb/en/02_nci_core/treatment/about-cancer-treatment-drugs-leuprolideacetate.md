## Use in Cancer

Leuprolide acetate is approved for [palliative treatment](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45815&version=Patient&language=English) of:

- **[prostate cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=445079&version=Patient&language=English)** that is [advanced](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=478743&version=Patient&language=English)

Leuprolide acetate is also being studied in the treatment of other types of [cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
