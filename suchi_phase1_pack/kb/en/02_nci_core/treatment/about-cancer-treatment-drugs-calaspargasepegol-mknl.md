## Use in Cancer

Calaspargase pegol-mknl
is approved to treat:

- **[Acute lymphoblastic leukemia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45586&version=Patient&language=English)** in children and young adults aged 1 month to 21 years. It is used with [combination chemotherapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45650&version=Patient&language=English).

Calaspargase pegol-mknl
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
[cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
