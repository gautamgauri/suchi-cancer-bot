## Use in Cancer

Glasdegib maleate is approved to treat:

- **[Acute myeloid leukemia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46347&version=Patient&language=English)** that is newly diagnosed. It is used with [low-dose](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=792090&version=Patient&language=English) [cytarabine](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45155&version=Patient&language=English) in patients aged 75 years and older or adults who cannot be treated with [intensive](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=796903&version=Patient&language=English) [induction](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45736&version=Patient&language=English) chemotherapy.

Glasdegib maleate
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
cancer.
