## Use in Cancer

Cosibelimab-ipdl
is approved to treat adults with:

- **[cutaneous squamous cell carcinoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=795104&version=Patient&language=English)** (a type of [skin cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=445084&version=Patient&language=English)) that has spread and cannot be cured with surgery or radiation therapy

Cosibelimab-ipdl
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
cancer.
