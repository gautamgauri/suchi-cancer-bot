## Use in Cancer

Dexamethasone
is approved
to be used to reduce [inflammation](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44042&version=Patient&language=English) and suppress (lower) the body's [immune response](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45722&version=Patient&language=English).

Dexamethasone is used with other [drugs](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=348921&version=Patient&language=English) to treat the following types of [cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English):

- **[Leukemia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45343&version=Patient&language=English).**
- **[Lymphoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45368&version=Patient&language=English).**
- **[Multiple myeloma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45793&version=Patient&language=English)**.
- **[Mycosis fungoides](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45794&version=Patient&language=English)** (a type of [cutaneous T-cell lymphoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46771&version=Patient&language=English)).

Dexamethasone is also used alone or with other drugs to [prevent](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=439419&version=Patient&language=English) or treat the following [conditions](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=651193&version=Patient&language=English) related to cancer:

- **[Anemia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45360&version=Patient&language=English).**
- **[Cerebral](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46482&version=Patient&language=English) [edema](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45676&version=Patient&language=English)** ([fluid](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44669&version=Patient&language=English) build-up in the brain) associated with [brain tumors](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=387264&version=Patient&language=English).
- **[Drug hypersensitivity](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45715&version=Patient&language=English)** ([allergic reactions](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=443292&version=Patient&language=English)).
- **[Hypercalcemia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45363&version=Patient&language=English)** (high [blood](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=270735&version=Patient&language=English) levels of [calcium](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45632&version=Patient&language=English)).
- **[Thrombocytopenia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45365&version=Patient&language=English)** (low [platelet](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45840&version=Patient&language=English) levels).

Dexamethasone
is also used alone or with other drugs to treat many other diseases and conditions. The drug continues to be studied in the treatment of many types of
cancer and other conditions.
