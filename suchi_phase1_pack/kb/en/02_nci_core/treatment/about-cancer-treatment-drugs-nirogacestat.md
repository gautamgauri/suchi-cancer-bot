## Use in Cancer

Nirogacestat hydrobromide is approved to treat:

- **[Desmoid tumors](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45390&version=Patient&language=English)** that have gotten worse. It is used in adults who need [systemic therapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45922&version=Patient&language=English).

Nirogacestat hydrobromide
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
cancer.
