## Use in Cancer

Tocilizumab
is approved to treat:

- [Cytokine release syndrome](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=791353&version=Patient&language=English) (CRS) that is severe or life-threatening. It is used in adults and children aged 2 years and older who have CRS caused by [chimeric antigen receptor (CAR) T-cell therapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=771301&version=Patient&language=English).

Tocilizumab
is also being studied in the treatment of other conditions.
