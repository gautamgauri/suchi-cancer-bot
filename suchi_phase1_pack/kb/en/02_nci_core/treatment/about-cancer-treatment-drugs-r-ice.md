# R-ICE - NCI

# R-ICE

This combination contains the targeted therapy drug rituximab and the chemotherapy drugs ifosfamide, carboplatin, and etoposide phosphate. Combinations usually work better than single drugs because
different drugs kill
cancer
cells in different ways.

Drugs in the R-ICE combination:

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **R** | = [Rituximab](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/rituximab) |
| **I** | = [Ifosfamide](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/ifosfamide) |
| **C** | = [Carboplatin](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/carboplatin) |
| **E** | = [Etoposide Phosphate](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/etoposidephosphate) |

Each of the drugs in this combination is approved by the
[Food and Drug
Administration (FDA)](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=454785&version=Patient&language=English)
to treat cancer or conditions related to cancer.

## Use in Cancer

R-ICE
is used to treat:

- **[Non-Hodgkin lymphoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45148&version=Patient&language=English)** (certain types).

This combination may also be used with other drugs or
treatments or to treat other types of cancer.
