## Use in Cancer

Propranolol hydrochloride
is approved to treat:

- **[Infantile hemangioma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=781614&version=Patient&language=English)** (a [benign tumor](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46079&version=Patient&language=English)). It is used in infants 5 weeks to 5 months of age. This use is approved for the Hemangeol brand of propranolol hydrochloride.

Propranolol hydrochloride is also used under other brand names to treat [hypertension](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44038&version=Patient&language=English), including before [surgery](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45570&version=Patient&language=English) for patients with [pheochromocytoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=322877&version=Patient&language=English), and other diseases and [conditions](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=651193&version=Patient&language=English). It is also being studied in the treatment of other conditions and types of
[cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
