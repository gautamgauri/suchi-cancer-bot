## Use in Cancer

Carfilzomib
is approved
to be used alone or with other [drugs](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=348921&version=Patient&language=English) to treat:

- **[Multiple myeloma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45793&version=Patient&language=English)** that has [relapsed](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45866&version=Patient&language=English) (come back) or is [refractory](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45863&version=Patient&language=English) (does not [respond](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44085&version=Patient&language=English) to treatment). It is used:
- Alone in adults who have received one or more other therapies.
- With other drugs in adults who have received one to three other therapies. It is used with [dexamethasone](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45262&version=Patient&language=English) with or without one of the following drugs:
- [Lenalidomide](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=393761&version=Patient&language=English)
- [Daratumumab](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=776992&version=Patient&language=English)
- [Daratumumab and hyaluronidase-fihj](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=801557&version=Patient&language=English)

Carfilzomib
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
[cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
