## Use in Cancer

Bicalutamide
is approved
to treat:

- **[Prostate cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=445079&version=Patient&language=English)** that has [metastasized](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46283&version=Patient&language=English) (spread to other parts of the body). It is used with a type of [drug](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=348921&version=Patient&language=English) called a [luteinizing hormone-releasing hormone agonist](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46304&version=Patient&language=English).

Bicalutamide
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
[cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
