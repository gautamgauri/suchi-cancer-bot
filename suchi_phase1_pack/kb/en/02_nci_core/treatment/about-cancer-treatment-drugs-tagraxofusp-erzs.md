## Use in Cancer

Tagraxofusp-erzs
is approved to treat:

- **[Blastic plasmacytoid dendritic cell neoplasm](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=796824&version=Patient&language=English)** (BPDCN) in adults and in children aged 2 years and older.

Tagraxofusp-erzs
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
cancer.
