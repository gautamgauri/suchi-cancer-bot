# Mosunetuzumab-axgb - NCI

# Mosunetuzumab-axgb

Placeholder slot

(moh-SUN-eh-TOO-zoo-mab)

Mosunetuzumab-axgb works by bringing healthy T cells (immune cells that help kill cancer cells) and lymphoma cells close together so the T cells can more effectively kill the lymphoma cells. It does this by binding to a protein called CD3 on healthy T cells and a protein called CD20 on B cells (the immune cells that are cancerous in B-cell lymphomas). Mosunetuzumab-axgb is a type of immunotherapy drug called a bispecific T-cell engager (BiTE).
