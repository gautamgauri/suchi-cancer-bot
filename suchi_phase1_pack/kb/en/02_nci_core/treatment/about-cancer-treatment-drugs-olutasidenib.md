## Use in Cancer

Olutasidenib
is approved to treat:

- **[Acute myeloid leukemia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46347&version=Patient&language=English)** (AML) that has a [mutation](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46063&version=Patient&language=English) in the *[IDH1](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=794150&version=Patient&language=English)* gene. It is used in adults whose cancer has come back or has not gotten better with other treatment.

Olutasidenib
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
cancer.
