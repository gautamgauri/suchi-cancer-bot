## Use in Cancer

Lutetium Lu 177 vipivotide tetraxetan
is approved to treat:

- **[Prostate cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=445079&version=Patient&language=English)** that produces high levels of the [PSMA](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=804938&version=Patient&language=English) protein, has not responded to treatments that lower [testosterone](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45581&version=Patient&language=English) levels, and has spread to other parts of the body. It is used in adults who received an [androgen receptor blocker](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=797801&version=Patient&language=English) and can either delay or have received treatment with [taxane](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46138&version=Patient&language=English)-based chemotherapy.
