## Use in Cancer

Bendamustine hydrochloride is approved to treat:

- **[B-cell](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=444967&version=Patient&language=English) [non-Hodgkin lymphoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45148&version=Patient&language=English)** (NHL) that is indolent (slow-growing) and got worse during or within 6 months after treatment with [rituximab](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45525&version=Patient&language=English).
- **[Chronic lymphocytic leukemia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44846&version=Patient&language=English)** (CLL).

Bendamustine hydrochloride is also being studied in the treatment of other types of cancer.
