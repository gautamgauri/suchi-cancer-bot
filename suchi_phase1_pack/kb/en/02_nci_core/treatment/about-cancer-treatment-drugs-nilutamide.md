## Use in Cancer

Nilutamide
is approved to treat:

- **[Prostate cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=445079&version=Patient&language=English)** that has [metastasized](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46283&version=Patient&language=English) (spread to other parts of the body). It is used in patients who have had [surgery](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45570&version=Patient&language=English) to remove the [testicles](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46611&version=Patient&language=English) ([orchiectomy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45441&version=Patient&language=English)).
