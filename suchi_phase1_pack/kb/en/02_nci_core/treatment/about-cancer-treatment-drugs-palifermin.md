## Use in Cancer

Palifermin is approved to [prevent](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=439419&version=Patient&language=English) and treat:

- **[Oral](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44068&version=Patient&language=English) [mucositis](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45789&version=Patient&language=English)** that is severe. It may be used in adults and children whose [blood cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=733834&version=Patient&language=English) is being treated with [high-dose chemotherapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=346522&version=Patient&language=English) and [radiation therapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44971&version=Patient&language=English), followed by [stem cell rescue](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=666718&version=Patient&language=English).

Palifermin is also being studied in the prevention and treatment of common [side effects](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46580&version=Patient&language=English) in other types of [cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
