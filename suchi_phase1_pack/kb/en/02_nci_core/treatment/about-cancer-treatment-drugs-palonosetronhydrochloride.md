## Use in Cancer

Palonosetron hydrochloride is approved in adults and children aged 1 month and older to [prevent](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=439419&version=Patient&language=English):

- **[Nausea](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=390302&version=Patient&language=English)** and **[vomiting](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=390324&version=Patient&language=English)** caused by [chemotherapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45214&version=Patient&language=English) and nausea and vomiting after [surgery](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45570&version=Patient&language=English).
