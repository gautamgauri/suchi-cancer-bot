## Use in Cancer

Tovorafenib
is approved to treat:

- **[Glioma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45700&version=Patient&language=English)** that is [low grade](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=386213&version=Patient&language=English), has come back or did not respond to treatment, and has an abnormal *[BRAF](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=561325&version=Patient&language=English)* gene. It is used in children aged 6 months and older.

Tovorafenib is approved under FDAâ€™s [Accelerated Approval Program](https://www.fda.gov/ForPatients/Approvals/Fast/ucm405447.htm). As a condition of approval, [confirmatory trial(s)](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=799146&version=Patient&language=English) must show that it provides a clinical benefit in these patients.

Tovorafenib
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
cancer.
