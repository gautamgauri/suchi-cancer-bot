## Use in Cancer

Necitumumab
is approved to be used with [gemcitabine hydrochloride](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=508951&version=Patient&language=English) and [cisplatin](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45230&version=Patient&language=English) to treat:

- **[Squamous](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46595&version=Patient&language=English) [non-small cell lung cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45323&version=Patient&language=English)** that has [metastasized](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46283&version=Patient&language=English) (spread to other parts of the body). It is used as [first-line treatment](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=346494&version=Patient&language=English).

Necitumumab
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
[cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
