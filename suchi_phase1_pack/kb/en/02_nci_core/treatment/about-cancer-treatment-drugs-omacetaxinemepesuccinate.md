## Use in Cancer

Omacetaxine mepesuccinate
is approved
to treat:

- **[Chronic myelogenous leukemia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44901&version=Patient&language=English).** It is used in adults with [chronic phase](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45160&version=Patient&language=English) or [accelerated phase](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46212&version=Patient&language=English) disease that cannot be treated with [tyrosine kinase inhibitors](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44833&version=Patient&language=English) or that has gotten worse after treatment with at least two tyrosine kinase inhibitors.

Omacetaxine mepesuccinate
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
[cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
