## Use in Cancer

Ondansetron hydrochloride
is approved
to [prevent](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=439419&version=Patient&language=English):

- **[Nausea](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=390302&version=Patient&language=English)** and **[vomiting](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=390324&version=Patient&language=English)** caused by [chemotherapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45214&version=Patient&language=English), [radiation therapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44971&version=Patient&language=English), and [surgery](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45570&version=Patient&language=English). It is used in adults and children.

Ondansetron hydrochloride
is also being studied in the treatment of other [conditions](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=651193&version=Patient&language=English).
