## Use in Cancer

Fludarabine phosphate is approved to treat adults with:

- **[B-cell](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45611&version=Patient&language=English) Â [chronic lymphocytic leukemia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44846&version=Patient&language=English) (CLL)**. It is used as part of combination therapy for B-cell CLL or alone for B-cell ALL that did not get better or got worse during at least one other treatment that included an [alkylating agent](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45589&version=Patient&language=English).

Fludarabine phosphate is also being studied in the treatment of other types of cancer.
