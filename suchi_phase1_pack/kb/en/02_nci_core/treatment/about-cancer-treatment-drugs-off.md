# OFF - NCI

# OFF

This chemotherapy combination contains the drugs oxaliplatin, fluorouracil, and leucovorin calcium (folinic acid). Combinations usually work better than single drugs because
different drugs kill
cancer
cells in different ways.

Drugs in the OFF combination:

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **O** | = [Oxaliplatin](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/oxaliplatin) |
| **F** | = [Fluorouracil](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/fluorouracil) |
| **F** | = [Leucovorin Calcium](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/leucovorincalcium) (Folinic Acid) |

Each of the drugs in this combination is approved by the
[Food and Drug
Administration (FDA)](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=454785&version=Patient&language=English)
to treat cancer or conditions related to cancer.

## Use in Cancer

OFF
is used to treat:

- **[Pancreatic cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44521&version=Patient&language=English)** that is [advanced](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=478743&version=Patient&language=English) and has gotten worse after treatment with [gemcitabine hydrochloride](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=508951&version=Patient&language=English).

This combination may also be used with other drugs or
treatments or to treat other types of cancer.
