# TPF - NCI

# TPF

This chemotherapy combination contains the drugs docetaxel (Taxotere), cisplatin (Platinol), and fluorouracil. Combinations usually work better than single drugs because
different drugs kill
cancer
cells in different ways.

Drugs in the TPF combination:

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **T** | = [Docetaxel](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/docetaxel) (Taxotere) |
| **P** | = [Cisplatin](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/cisplatin) (Platinol) |
| **F** | = [Fluorouracil](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/fluorouracil) |

Each of the drugs in this combination is approved by the
[Food and Drug
Administration (FDA)](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=454785&version=Patient&language=English)
to treat cancer or conditions related to cancer.

## Use in Cancer

TPF
is used to treat:

- **[Gastric (stomach) cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=454513&version=Patient&language=English)** that is [advanced](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=478743&version=Patient&language=English).
- **[Squamous cell carcinoma of the head and neck](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=597171&version=Patient&language=English)** that is [locally advanced](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45955&version=Patient&language=English).

This combination may also be used with other drugs or
treatments or to treat other types of cancer.
