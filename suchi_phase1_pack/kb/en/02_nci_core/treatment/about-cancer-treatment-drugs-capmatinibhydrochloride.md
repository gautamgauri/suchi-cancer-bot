## Use in Cancer

Capmatinib hydrochloride
is approved to treat:

- **[Non-small cell lung cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45323&version=Patient&language=English)** that has a certain [mutation](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46063&version=Patient&language=English) in the *[MET](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=794425&version=Patient&language=English)* gene. It is used in adults whose cancer has spread to other parts of the body.

Capmatinib hydrochloride
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
cancer.
