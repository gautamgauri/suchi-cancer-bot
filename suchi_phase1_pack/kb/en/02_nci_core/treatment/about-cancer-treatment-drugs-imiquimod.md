## Use in Cancer

Imiquimod is approved to treat:

- **[Actinic keratosis](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45133&version=Patient&language=English)** in [immunocompetent](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44835&version=Patient&language=English) adults.This use is approved for the Aldara and Zyclara brands of imiquimod.
- **[Basal cell carcinoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46515&version=Patient&language=English)** that is [superficial](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44305&version=Patient&language=English). It is used in immunocompetent adults. This use is approved for the Aldara brand of imiquimod.
- **[Genital warts](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=409765&version=Patient&language=English) or [perianal warts](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=801199&version=Patient&language=English)** in adults and in children aged 12 years and older. This use is approved for the Aldara and Zyclara brands of imiquimod.

Imiquimod is also being studied in the treatment of other conditions and types of [cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
