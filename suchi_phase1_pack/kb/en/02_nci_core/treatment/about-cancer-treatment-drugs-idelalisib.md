## Use in Cancer

Idelalisib
is approved to treat:

- **[Chronic lymphocytic leukemia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44846&version=Patient&language=English)** (CLL) that has [relapsed](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45866&version=Patient&language=English) (come back). It is used with [rituximab](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45525&version=Patient&language=English) in patients who may be treated with rituximab alone due to other health problems.

Idelalisib
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
[cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
