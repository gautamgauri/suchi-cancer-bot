## Use in Cancer

Caplacizumab-yhdp
is approved to treat:

- **[Acquired thrombotic thrombocytopenic purpura](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=797028&version=Patient&language=English)** (aTTP) in adults. It is used with [plasma exchange](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=796984&version=Patient&language=English) and [immunosuppressive therapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45728&version=Patient&language=English).
