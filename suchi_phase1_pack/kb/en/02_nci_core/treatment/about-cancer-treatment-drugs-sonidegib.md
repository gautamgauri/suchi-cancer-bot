## Use in Cancer

Sonidegib
is approved
to treat:

- **[Basal cell carcinoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46515&version=Patient&language=English)** that is [locally advanced](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45955&version=Patient&language=English). It is used in adults whose disease has [recurred](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45862&version=Patient&language=English) (come back) after [surgery](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45570&version=Patient&language=English) or [radiation therapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44971&version=Patient&language=English) or who cannot be treated with surgery or radiation therapy.

Sonidegib
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
[cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
