# CVP - NCI

# CVP

This chemotherapy combination contains the chemotherapy drugs cyclophosphamide and vincristine sulfate and the steroid hormone prednisone. Combinations usually work better than single drugs because
different drugs kill
cancer
cells in different ways.

Drugs in the CVP combination:

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **C** | = [Cyclophosphamide](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/cyclophosphamide) |
| **V** | = [Vincristine Sulfate](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/vincristinesulfate) |
| **P** | = [Prednisone](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/prednisone) |

Each of the
drugs
in this
combination
is approved by the
[Food and Drug
Administration (FDA)](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=454785&version=Patient&language=English)
to treat
cancer
or conditions related to cancer.

## Use in Cancer

CVP is used to treat:

- **[Chronic lymphocytic leukemia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44846&version=Patient&language=English)** (CLL).
- **[Non-Hodgkin lymphoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45148&version=Patient&language=English)** (NHL) that is [indolent](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45735&version=Patient&language=English) (slow-growing).

This combination may also be used with other drugs or treatments or to treat other types of cancer.
