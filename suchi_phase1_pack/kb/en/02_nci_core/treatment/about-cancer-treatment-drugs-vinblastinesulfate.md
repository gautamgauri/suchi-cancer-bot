# Vinblastine Sulfate - NCI

# Vinblastine Sulfate

Placeholder slot

(vin-BLAS-teen SUL-fayt)

Vinblastine sulfate is a type of chemotherapy called a vinca alkaloid. Vinca alkaloids interfere with microtubules (cellular structures that help move chromosomes during mitosis). They block cell growth by stopping mitosis (cell division).

## Use in Cancer

Vinblastine sulfate
is approved as [palliative treatment](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45815&version=Patient&language=English) for:

- **[Breast cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=444971&version=Patient&language=English)** that has not gotten better with other treatment. It is used in adults.
- **[Choriocarcinoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46753&version=Patient&language=English)** (a type of [gestational trophoblastic disease](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46199&version=Patient&language=English)) that has not gotten better with other [chemotherapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45214&version=Patient&language=English). It is used in adults.
- **[Hodgkin lymphoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=270800&version=Patient&language=English).** It is used in adults and children with [advanced](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=478743&version=Patient&language=English) disease.
- **[Kaposi sarcoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45134&version=Patient&language=English).** It is used in adults.
- **[Mycosis fungoides](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45794&version=Patient&language=English)** (a type of [cutaneous T-cell lymphoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46771&version=Patient&language=English)). It is used in adults.
- **[Non-Hodgkin lymphoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45148&version=Patient&language=English)** (NHL) in adults.
- **[Testicular germ cell tumors](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=695185&version=Patient&language=English).** It is used in adults and children with advanced disease.
