## Use in Cancer

Ripretinib
is approved to treat:

- **[Gastrointestinal stromal tumor](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44998&version=Patient&language=English)** that is [advanced](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=478743&version=Patient&language=English). It is used in adults whose cancer has been treated with at least three [kinase inhibitors](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=750798&version=Patient&language=English), including [imatinib mesylate](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44893&version=Patient&language=English).

Ripretinib
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
[cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
