## Use in Cancer

Mesna
is approved to [prevent](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=439419&version=Patient&language=English):

- **[Hemorrhagic cystitis](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=695987&version=Patient&language=English)** (bleeding in the [bladder](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46501&version=Patient&language=English)) caused by [chemotherapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45214&version=Patient&language=English) with [ifosfamide](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45267&version=Patient&language=English).
