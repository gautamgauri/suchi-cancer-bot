# Drugs Approved for Lung Cancer - NCI

# Drugs Approved for Lung Cancer

This page lists cancer drugs approved by the Food and Drug Administration (FDA) for lung cancer. The list includes generic and brand names. This page also lists common drug combinations used in lung cancer. The individual drugs in the combinations are FDA-approved. However, the drug combinations themselves usually are not approved, although they are widely used.

The drug names link to NCI's Cancer Drug Information summaries. There may be drugs used in lung cancer that are not listed here.
