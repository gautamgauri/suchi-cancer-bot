## Use in Cancer

Pegaspargase is approved to be used with other drugs to treat:

- **[Acute lymphoblastic leukemia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45586&version=Patient&language=English)** (ALL). It is used in adults and children:
- As the first treatment.
- In patients whose cancer cannot be treated with [asparaginase](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45339&version=Patient&language=English).

Pegaspargase is also being studied in the treatment of other types of cancer.
