## Use in Cancer

Decitabine is approved to treat adults with:

- **[Myelodysplastic syndromes](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45266&version=Patient&language=English)** (MDS), including [chronic myelomonocytic leukemia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=367436&version=Patient&language=English) (CMML).

Decitabine is also being studied in the treatment of other conditions and types of [cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).

Decitabine is also available in a tablet form. For more information, see the Drug Information Summary for [Decitabine and Cedazuridine](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/decitabineandcedazuridine).
