# Drugs Approved for Esophageal Cancer - NCI

# Drugs Approved for Esophageal Cancer

This page lists cancer drugs approved by the Food and Drug Administration (FDA) for esophageal cancer, including gastroesophageal junction cancer. The list includes generic names and brand names. The drug names link to NCI's Cancer Drug Information summaries. There may be drugs used in esophageal cancer that are not listed here.
