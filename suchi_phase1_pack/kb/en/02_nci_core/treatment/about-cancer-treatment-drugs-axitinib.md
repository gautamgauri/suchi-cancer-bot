## Use in Cancer

Axitinib
is approved
to treat:

- **[Renal cell carcinoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=661352&version=Patient&language=English)** (a type of [kidney cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=444995&version=Patient&language=English)) that is [advanced](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=478743&version=Patient&language=English). It is used:
- With [avelumab](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=787903&version=Patient&language=English) or [pembrolizumab](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=764538&version=Patient&language=English) as the first treatment.
- Alone in patients whose cancer did not get better after one treatment with [systemic therapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45922&version=Patient&language=English).

Axitinib
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
cancer.
