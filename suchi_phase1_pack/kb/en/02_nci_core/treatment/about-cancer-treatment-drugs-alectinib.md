## Use in Cancer

Alectinib
is approved to treat adults with:

- **[Non-small cell lung cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45323&version=Patient&language=English)**(NSCLC) that is [ALK positive](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=796880&version=Patient&language=English). It is used:
- To prevent NSCLC from coming back after it was removed by surgery.
- In patients whose NSCLC has spread to other parts of the body.
