# GEMCITABINE-CISPLATIN - NCI

# GEMCITABINE-CISPLATIN

Placeholder slot

(jem-SY-tuh-been-sis-PLA-tin)

This chemotherapy combination contains the drugs gemcitabine and cisplatin. Combinations usually work better than single drugs because
different drugs kill
cancer
cells in different ways.

Drugs in the GEMCITABINE-CISPLATIN combination:

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
|  | [Gemcitabine Hydrochloride](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/gemcitabinehydrochloride) |
|  | [Cisplatin](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/cisplatin) |

Each of the drugs in this combination is approved by the [Food and Drug Administration (FDA)](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=454785&version=Patient&language=English) to treat cancer or conditions related to cancer.

## Use in Cancer

GEMCITABINE-CISPLATIN is used to treat:

- **[Biliary tract cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=559456&version=Patient&language=English).**
- **[Bladder cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=444968&version=Patient&language=English).**
- **[Cervical cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=444973&version=Patient&language=English).**
- **[Malignant mesothelioma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44992&version=Patient&language=English).**
- **[Non-small cell lung cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45323&version=Patient&language=English)** (NSCLC).
- **[Ovarian cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=445074&version=Patient&language=English).**
- **[Pancreatic cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44521&version=Patient&language=English).**

This combination may also be used with other drugs or treatments or to treat other types of cancer.
