## Use in Cancer

Futibatinib
is approved to treat:

- **Intrahepatic [cholangiocarcinoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=335064&version=Patient&language=English)** (cancer that forms in the bile ducts inside the liver) that has spread or cannot be removed with surgery. It is used in adults whose cancer has been treated and has an *[FGFR2](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=798333&version=Patient&language=English)* [gene fusion](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=613509&version=Patient&language=English) or other change in the structure of the *FGFR2* gene.

Futibatinib is approved under FDAâ€™s [Accelerated Approval Program](https://www.fda.gov/ForPatients/Approvals/Fast/ucm405447.htm). As a condition of approval, [confirmatory trial(s)](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=799146&version=Patient&language=English) must show that it provides a clinical benefit in these patients.

Futibatinib
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
cancer.
