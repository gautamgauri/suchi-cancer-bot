# PCV - NCI

# PCV

This chemotherapy combination contains the drugs procarbazine hydrochloride, lomustine (CCNU), and vincristine sulfate. Combinations usually work better than single drugs because
different drugs kill
cancer
cells in different ways.

Drugs in the PCV combination:

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **P** | = [Procarbazine Hydrochloride](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/procarbazinehydrochloride) |
| **C** | = [Lomustine](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/lomustine) (CCNU) |
| **V** | = [Vincristine Sulfate](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/vincristinesulfate) |

Each of the drugs in this combination is approved by the
[Food and Drug
Administration (FDA)](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=454785&version=Patient&language=English)
to treat cancer or conditions related to cancer.

## Use in Cancer

PCV
is used to treat:

- **[Brain tumors](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=387264&version=Patient&language=English)** (certain types). It is often used with [radiation therapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44971&version=Patient&language=English).

This combination may also be used with other drugs or
treatments or to treat other types of cancer.
