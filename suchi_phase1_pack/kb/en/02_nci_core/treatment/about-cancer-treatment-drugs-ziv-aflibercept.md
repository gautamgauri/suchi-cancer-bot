## Use in Cancer

Ziv-aflibercept
is approved
to be used with [FOLFIRI](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=635807&version=Patient&language=English) to treat:

- **[Colorectal cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=444983&version=Patient&language=English)** that has [metastasized](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46283&version=Patient&language=English) (spread to other parts of the body). It is used in patients whose disease has not gotten better with other [chemotherapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45214&version=Patient&language=English).

Ziv-aflibercept
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
[cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
