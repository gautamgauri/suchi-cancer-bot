## Use in Cancer

Alemtuzumab is approved to treat:

- **[B-cell](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45611&version=Patient&language=English) [chronic lymphocytic leukemia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44846&version=Patient&language=English)** (CLL).

Alemtuzumab (Campath) is only available as part of a special program called U.S. Campath Distribution Program.

Alemtuzumab is also being studied in the treatment of other conditions and types of [cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
