## Use in Cancer

Chlorambucil
is approved for [palliative treatment](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45815&version=Patient&language=English) of:

- **[Chronic lymphocytic leukemia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44846&version=Patient&language=English).**
- **[Hodgkin lymphoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=270800&version=Patient&language=English).**
- **[Non-Hodgkin lymphoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45148&version=Patient&language=English)** (certain types).

Chlorambucil is also being studied in the treatment of other types of [cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
