## Use in Cancer

Zoledronic acid is approved to be used alone or with other drugs to treat:

- **[multiple myeloma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45793&version=Patient&language=English)** and cancers that have spread to the bone
- **[hypercalcemia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45363&version=Patient&language=English)** (high blood levels of [calcium](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45632&version=Patient&language=English)) caused by [malignant](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45772&version=Patient&language=English) cancer

Zoledronic acid is being studied in the treatment of other types of cancer.
