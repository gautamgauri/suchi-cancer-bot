## Use in Cancer

Elotuzumab
is approved to treat:

- **[Multiple myeloma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45793&version=Patient&language=English).** It is used:
- With [lenalidomide](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=393761&version=Patient&language=English) and [dexamethasone](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45262&version=Patient&language=English) in adults who have received one to three other types of treatment.
- With [pomalidomide](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=589401&version=Patient&language=English) and dexamethasone in adults who have received at least two other therapies, including lenalidomide and a [proteasome inhibitor](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=393541&version=Patient&language=English).
