## Use in Cancer

Siltuximab
is approved
to treat:

- **Multicentric [Castleman disease](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46043&version=Patient&language=English)** in patients who do not have [human immunodeficiency virus](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44366&version=Patient&language=English) (HIV) or [human herpesvirus 8](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=430695&version=Patient&language=English) (HHV8).

Siltuximab
is also being studied in the treatment of several types of
[cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
