## Use in Cancer

Rasburicase is approved to treat adults and children with:

- **High levels of [uric acid](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=393536&version=Patient&language=English) in the [blood](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=270735&version=Patient&language=English).**

Rasburicase is also approved to [prevent](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=439419&version=Patient&language=English):

- **[Tumor lysis syndrome](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=626342&version=Patient&language=English)** in adults and children with [leukemia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45343&version=Patient&language=English), [lymphoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45368&version=Patient&language=English), or [malignant](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45772&version=Patient&language=English) [solid tumors](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45301&version=Patient&language=English). It is used when the treatment is expected to cause high blood levels of uric acid.
