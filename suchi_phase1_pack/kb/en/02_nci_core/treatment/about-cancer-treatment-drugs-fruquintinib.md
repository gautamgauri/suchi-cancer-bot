## Use in Cancer

Fruquintinib
is approved to treat:

- **[Colorectal cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=444983&version=Patient&language=English)** that has spread to other parts of the body. It is used in adults who have received previous treatment with a [fluoropyrimidine](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=386207&version=Patient&language=English), [oxaliplatin](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45465&version=Patient&language=English), [irinotecan hydrochloride](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=635811&version=Patient&language=English), and an anti-[VEGF](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44222&version=Patient&language=English) therapy. Patients whose cancer has the [wild-type KRAS gene](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=785884&version=Patient&language=English) may also have received an anti-[EGFR](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44397&version=Patient&language=English) therapy.

Fruquintinib
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
cancer.
