## Use in Cancer

Lanreotide acetate
is approved
to treat:

- **[Gastroenteropancreatic neuroendocrine tumors](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=792398&version=Patient&language=English).** It is used in some adults whose tumors cannot be removed by [surgery](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45570&version=Patient&language=English), are [locally advanced](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45955&version=Patient&language=English), or have [metastasized](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46283&version=Patient&language=English) (spread to other parts of the body).
- **[Carcinoid syndrome](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=339609&version=Patient&language=English)** in adults.

Lanreotide acetate
is also being studied in the treatment of other conditions or types of
[cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
