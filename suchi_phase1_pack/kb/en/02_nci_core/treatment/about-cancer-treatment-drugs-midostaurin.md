## Use in Cancer

Midostaurin
is approved to treat:

- **[Acute myeloid leukemia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46347&version=Patient&language=English)** (AML). It is used with [cytarabine](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45155&version=Patient&language=English) and [daunorubicin hydrochloride](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=583859&version=Patient&language=English) in newly diagnosed adults whose cancer has a [mutation](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46063&version=Patient&language=English) in the *[FLT3](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=789010&version=Patient&language=English)* gene.
- **[Aggressive systemic mastocytosis](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=789073&version=Patient&language=English)**, **[systemic mastocytosis with associated hematologic neoplasm](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=789076&version=Patient&language=English)** (SM-AHN), or **[mast cell leukemia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=789079&version=Patient&language=English)** in adults.

Midostaurin
is also being studied in the treatment of other conditions and types of
cancer.
