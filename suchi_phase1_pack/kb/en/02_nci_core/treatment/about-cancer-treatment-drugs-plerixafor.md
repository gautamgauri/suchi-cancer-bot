## Use in Cancer

Plerixafor is approved to be used with [granulocyte colony-stimulating factor](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46749&version=Patient&language=English) (G-CSF) for:

- **[Multiple myeloma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45793&version=Patient&language=English).**
- **[Non-Hodgkin lymphoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45148&version=Patient&language=English)** (NHL).

Plerixafor helps move [stem cells](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46598&version=Patient&language=English) from the [bone marrow](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45622&version=Patient&language=English) to the bloodstream so that they can be collected, stored, and then given back to the patient in [autologous stem cell transplantation](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=270733&version=Patient&language=English).

Plerixafor is also being studied in the treatment of other types of cancer.
