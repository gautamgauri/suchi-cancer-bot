## Use in Cancer

Ensartinib hydrochloride
is approved to treat:

- **[Non-small cell lung cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45323&version=Patient&language=English)** that is [ALK positive](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=796880&version=Patient&language=English) and has spread. It is used in adults whose cancer has not been treated with an [ALK inhibitor](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=795666&version=Patient&language=English).

Ensartinib hydrochloride
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
cancer.
