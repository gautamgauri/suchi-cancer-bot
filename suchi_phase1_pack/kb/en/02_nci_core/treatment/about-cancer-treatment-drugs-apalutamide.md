## Use in Cancer

Apalutamide
is approved to treat:

- **[Prostate cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=445079&version=Patient&language=English).** It is used:
- In patients whose cancer has [metastasized](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46283&version=Patient&language=English) (spread to other parts of the body) and is [castrate sensitive](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=804591&version=Patient&language=English) (has [responded](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44085&version=Patient&language=English) to treatments that lower [testosterone](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45581&version=Patient&language=English) levels).
- In patients whose cancer has not metastasized and is [castrate resistant](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=753525&version=Patient&language=English) (has not responded to treatments that lower testosterone levels).
