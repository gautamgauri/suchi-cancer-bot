## Use in Cancer

Bortezomib is approved to treat adults with:

- **[Mantle cell lymphoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=445048&version=Patient&language=English)** in patients who have received at least one other type of treatment.
- **[Multiple myeloma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45793&version=Patient&language=English).**

Bortezomib is also being studied in the treatment of other types of [cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
