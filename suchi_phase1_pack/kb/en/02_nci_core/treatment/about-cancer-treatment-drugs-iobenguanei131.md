## Use in Cancer

Iobenguane I 131
is approved to treat:

- **[Pheochromocytoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=322877&version=Patient&language=English)** or **[paraganglioma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=390305&version=Patient&language=English)** that is [malignant](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45772&version=Patient&language=English) and requires [systemic](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45922&version=Patient&language=English) [anticancer therapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=639945&version=Patient&language=English). It is used in adults and children aged 12 years or older whose iobenguane [scan](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46570&version=Patient&language=English) was [positive](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44710&version=Patient&language=English) and whose disease is [locally advanced](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45955&version=Patient&language=English), has spread to other parts of the body, or cannot be removed by [surgery](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45570&version=Patient&language=English).

Iobenguane I 131
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
[cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
