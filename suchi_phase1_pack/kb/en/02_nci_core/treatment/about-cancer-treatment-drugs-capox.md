# CAPOX

This chemotherapy combination contains the drugs capecitabine and oxaliplatin. Combinations usually work better than single drugs because
different drugs kill
cancer
cells in different ways.

Drugs in the CAPOX combination:

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **CAP** | = [Capecitabine](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/capecitabine) |
| **OX** | = [Oxaliplatin](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/oxaliplatin) |

Each of the drugs in this combination is approved by the
[Food and Drug
Administration (FDA)](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=454785&version=Patient&language=English)
to treat cancer or conditions related to cancer.

## Use in Cancer

CAPOX
is used to treat:

- **[Colorectal cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=444983&version=Patient&language=English)** that is [advanced](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=478743&version=Patient&language=English).

This combination may also be used with other drugs or treatments or to treat other types of cancer.
