## Use in Cancer

Vismodegib
is approved
to treat:

- **[Basal cell carcinoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46515&version=Patient&language=English)** (a type of [skin cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=445084&version=Patient&language=English)). It is used in adults whose disease has [metastasized](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46283&version=Patient&language=English) (spread to other parts of the body). It is also used in adults with [locally advanced](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45955&version=Patient&language=English) disease that has [recurred](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45862&version=Patient&language=English) (come back) after [surgery](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45570&version=Patient&language=English) or who cannot be treated with surgery or [radiation](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44971&version=Patient&language=English).

Vismodegib
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
[cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
