## Use in Cancer

Lenalidomide is approved to treat:

- **[Anemia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45360&version=Patient&language=English)** caused by certain types of [myelodysplastic syndromes](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45266&version=Patient&language=English). It is used in adults.
- **[Follicular lymphoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=428287&version=Patient&language=English)** in adults who have already received other treatment. It is used with [rituximab](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45525&version=Patient&language=English).
- **[Mantle cell lymphoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=445048&version=Patient&language=English)** in adults whose disease came back or got worse after two other treatments, including [bortezomib](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=269133&version=Patient&language=English).
- **[Marginal zone lymphoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=543124&version=Patient&language=English)** in adults who have already received other treatment. It is used with rituximab.
- **[Multiple myeloma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45793&version=Patient&language=English)** in adults. It is given with [dexamethasone](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45262&version=Patient&language=English). It is also used alone as [maintenance therapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45768&version=Patient&language=English) in adults who received an [autologous stem cell transplant](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=270733&version=Patient&language=English).

Lenalidomide is only available as part of a special program called Revlimid REMS ([Risk Evaluation and Mitigation Strategies](https://www.revlimidrems.com/patient.html)).

Lenalidomide is also being studied in the treatment of other conditions and types of cancer.
