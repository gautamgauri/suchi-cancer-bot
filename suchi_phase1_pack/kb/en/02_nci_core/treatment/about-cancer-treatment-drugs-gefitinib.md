## Use in Cancer

Gefitinib is approved to treat:

- **[Non-small cell lung cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45323&version=Patient&language=English)** (NSCLC) that has [metastasized](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46283&version=Patient&language=English) (spread to other parts of the body). It is used as [first-line treatment](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=346494&version=Patient&language=English) in patients whose tumors have certain *[EGFR](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=796876&version=Patient&language=English)* [gene](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45693&version=Patient&language=English) [mutations](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46063&version=Patient&language=English).

Gefitinib is also being studied in the treatment of other types of [cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
