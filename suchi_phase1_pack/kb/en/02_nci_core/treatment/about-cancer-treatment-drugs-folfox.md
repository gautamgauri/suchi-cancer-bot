This chemotherapy combination contains the drugs leucovorin calcium (folinic acid), fluorouracil, and oxaliplatin. Combinations usually work better than single drugs because
different drugs kill
cancer
cells in different ways.

Drugs in the FOLFOX combination:

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **FOL** | = [Leucovorin Calcium](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/leucovorincalcium) (Folinic Acid) |
| **F** | = [Fluorouracil](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/fluorouracil) |
| **OX** | = [Oxaliplatin](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/oxaliplatin) |

Each of the drugs in this combination is approved by the [Food and Drug Administration (FDA)](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=454785&version=Patient&language=English) to treat cancer or conditions related to cancer.

## Use in Cancer

FOLFOX is used to treat:

- **[Colorectal cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=444983&version=Patient&language=English).**

This combination may also be used with other drugs or treatments or to treat other types of cancer.
