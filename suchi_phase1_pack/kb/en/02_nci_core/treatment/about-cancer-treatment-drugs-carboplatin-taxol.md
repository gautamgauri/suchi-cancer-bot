# CARBOPLATIN-TAXOL - NCI

# CARBOPLATIN-TAXOL

Placeholder slot

(KAR-boh-pla-tin-TAK-sol)

This chemotherapy combination contains the drugs carboplatin and paclitaxel (Taxol). Combinations usually work better than single drugs because
different drugs kill
cancer
cells in different ways.

Drugs in the CARBOPLATIN-TAXOL combination:

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
|  | [Carboplatin](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/carboplatin) |
|  | [Paclitaxel](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/paclitaxel) (Taxol) |

Each of the drugs in this combination is approved by the
[Food and Drug
Administration (FDA)](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=454785&version=Patient&language=English)
to treat cancer or conditions related to cancer.

## Use in Cancer

CARBOPLATIN-TAXOL
is used to treat:

- **[Carcinoma of unknown primary](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=527198&version=Patient&language=English).**
- **[Cervical cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=444973&version=Patient&language=English).**
- **[Endometrial cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=444987&version=Patient&language=English).**
- **[Non-small cell lung cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45323&version=Patient&language=English).**
- **[Ovarian cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=445074&version=Patient&language=English).**
- **[Thymoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46002&version=Patient&language=English) or [thymic carcinoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=455462&version=Patient&language=English)** that is [advanced](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=478743&version=Patient&language=English) and has not been treated.

This combination may also be used with other drugs or treatments or to treat other types of cancer.
