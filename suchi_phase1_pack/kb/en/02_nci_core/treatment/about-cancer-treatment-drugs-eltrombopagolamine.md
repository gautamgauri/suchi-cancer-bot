## Use in Cancer

Eltrombopag olamine is approved to treat:

- **[Thrombocytopenia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45365&version=Patient&language=English)** (low [platelet](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45840&version=Patient&language=English) levels). It is used in adults and in children aged 1 year or older with [chronic](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45641&version=Patient&language=English) [immune thrombocytopenia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=793904&version=Patient&language=English) (ITP). ITP is a condition in which platelets are destroyed by the [immune system](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46356&version=Patient&language=English). Eltrombopag olamine is used in certain patients with ITP who have not gotten better with other treatment.

Eltrombopag olamine is also being studied in the treatment of other conditions and types of [cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
