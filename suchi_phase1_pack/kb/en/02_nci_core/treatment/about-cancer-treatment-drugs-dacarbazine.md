## Use in Cancer

Dacarbazine is approved to be used alone or with other [drugs](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=348921&version=Patient&language=English) to treat:

- **[Hodgkin lymphoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=270800&version=Patient&language=English)** in patients whose disease has not gotten better with other [chemotherapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45214&version=Patient&language=English).
- **[Melanoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45135&version=Patient&language=English)** that has [metastasized](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46283&version=Patient&language=English) (spread to other parts of the body).

Dacarbazine is also being studied in the treatment of other types of [cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
