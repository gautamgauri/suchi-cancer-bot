## Use in Cancer

Nelarabine is approved to treat adults and children aged 1 year or older with:

- **[T-cell acute lymphoblastic leukemia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=509731&version=Patient&language=English).**
- **[T-cell lymphoblastic lymphoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=509758&version=Patient&language=English).**

Nelarabine is used in patients whose cancer has not gotten better with other treatment or has come back after at least two chemotherapy [regimens](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45864&version=Patient&language=English).
