## Use in Cancer

Ravulizumab-cwvz
is approved to treat:

- **Atypical hemolytic uremic syndrome** (a [blood clotting](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=476017&version=Patient&language=English) disorder) in adults and children aged 1 month and older.
- **[Paroxysmal nocturnal hemoglobinuria](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=438734&version=Patient&language=English)** (PNH) in adults.

Ravulizumab-cwvz
is also being studied in the treatment of other conditions.
