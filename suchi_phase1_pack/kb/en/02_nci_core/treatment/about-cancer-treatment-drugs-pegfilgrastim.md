## Use in Cancer

Pegfilgrastim
is approved to reduce the chance of infection in adults and children with:

- **[Neutropenia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45361&version=Patient&language=English)** caused by [myelosuppressive](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45796&version=Patient&language=English) [chemotherapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45214&version=Patient&language=English) for non-[myeloid](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46279&version=Patient&language=English) [cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).

Pegfilgrastim is a form of [filgrastim](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45332&version=Patient&language=English) that has [polyethylene glycol](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=643072&version=Patient&language=English) (PEG) attached to it. This form stays in the body longer than does filgrastim, so it doesnâ€™t need to be given as often.
