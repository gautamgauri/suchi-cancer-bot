## Use in Cancer

Dactinomycin
is approved
to be used alone or with other [drugs](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=348921&version=Patient&language=English) to treat:

- **[Ewing sarcoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46031&version=Patient&language=English)** in adults and children.
- **[Gestational trophoblastic disease](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46199&version=Patient&language=English)** in adults and children.
- **[Rhabdomyosarcoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45872&version=Patient&language=English)** in adults and children.
- **[Solid tumors](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45301&version=Patient&language=English)** that are [locally recurrent](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=559442&version=Patient&language=English). It is used as [palliative](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45815&version=Patient&language=English) and/or [adjuvant therapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45587&version=Patient&language=English) in adults.
- **[Testicular cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=445090&version=Patient&language=English).** It is used in adults and children whose [nonseminomas](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46255&version=Patient&language=English) have [metastasized](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46283&version=Patient&language=English) (spread to other parts of the body).
- **[Wilms tumor](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45946&version=Patient&language=English)** in adults and children.

Dactinomycin
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
[cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
