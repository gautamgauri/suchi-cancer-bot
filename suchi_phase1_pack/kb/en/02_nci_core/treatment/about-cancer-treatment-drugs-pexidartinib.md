## Use in Cancer

Pexidartinib
hydrochloride is approved to treat:

- **[Tenosynovial giant cell tumor](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=801209&version=Patient&language=English).** It is used in certain adults who have [symptoms](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45022&version=Patient&language=English) associated with a severe form of the disease that is not likely to get better with [surgery](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45570&version=Patient&language=English).

Pexidartinib
hydrochloride is only available as part of a special program called Turalio REMS ([Risk Evaluation and Mitigation](https://www.turaliorems.com/#Main)).

Pexidartinib
hydrochloride
is also being studied in the treatment of some types of [cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English) and other conditions.
