## Use in Cancer

Dinutuximab
is approved
to be used with [granulocyte-macrophage colony-stimulating factor](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=423043&version=Patient&language=English) (GM-CSF), [aldesleukin](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46679&version=Patient&language=English) (IL-2), and [13-cis retinoic acid](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44428&version=Patient&language=English) to treat:

- **[Neuroblastoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45418&version=Patient&language=English)** that is [high risk](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=711109&version=Patient&language=English). It is used in children who had at least a [partial response](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45819&version=Patient&language=English) to other drugs and other types of treatment.
