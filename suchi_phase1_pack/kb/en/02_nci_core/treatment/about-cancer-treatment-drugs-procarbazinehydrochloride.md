## Use in Cancer

Procarbazine hydrochloride
is approved to be used with other [drugs](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=348921&version=Patient&language=English) to treat:

- **[Hodgkin lymphoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=270800&version=Patient&language=English)** that is [advanced](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=478743&version=Patient&language=English). It is used in adults and children.

Procarbazine
hydrochloride is also being studied in the treatment of other types of [cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
