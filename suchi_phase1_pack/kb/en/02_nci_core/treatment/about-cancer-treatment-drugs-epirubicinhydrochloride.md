# Epirubicin Hydrochloride - NCI

# Epirubicin Hydrochloride

Placeholder slot

(EH-pih-ROO-bih-sin HY-droh-KLOR-ide)

Epirubicin hydrochloride is a type of antibiotic called an anthracycline that is used as a chemotherapy drug. It stops cells from copying their DNA and from making RNA and proteins. This slows or stops the growth of cancer cells and other rapidly growing cells.

## Use in Cancer

Epirubicin hydrochloride is approved to be used with other drugs to treat:

- **[Breast cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=444971&version=Patient&language=English).** It is used after surgery in patients whose cancer has spread to the [lymph nodes](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45762&version=Patient&language=English) under the arm.

Epirubicin hydrochloride is also being studied in the treatment of other types of cancer.
