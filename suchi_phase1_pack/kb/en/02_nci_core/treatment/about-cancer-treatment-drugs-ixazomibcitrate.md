## Use in Cancer

Ixazomib citrate
is approved to be used with [lenalidomide](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=393761&version=Patient&language=English) and [dexamethasone](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45262&version=Patient&language=English) to treat:

- **[Multiple myeloma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45793&version=Patient&language=English)** in patients who have already received at least one other type of treatment.

Ixazomib citrate
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
[cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
