## Use in Cancer

Bexarotene is approved to treat:

- **Skin problems caused by [cutaneous T-cell lymphoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46771&version=Patient&language=English)** that cannot be treated with or that did not [respond](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44085&version=Patient&language=English) to other treatment.

Bexarotene is also being studied in the treatment of other types of [cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
