## Use in Cancer

Valrubicin is approved to treat:

**[Bladder cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=444968&version=Patient&language=English).** It is given directly into the [bladder](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46501&version=Patient&language=English) as a treatment for [carcinoma in situ](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46488&version=Patient&language=English) that does not [respond](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44085&version=Patient&language=English) to treatment with [bacillus Calmette-Gu√©rin](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44581&version=Patient&language=English) (BCG). It is used in patients who cannot have [surgery](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45570&version=Patient&language=English) right away.
