## Use in Cancer

Romiplostim is approved to treat:

- **[Thrombocytopenia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45365&version=Patient&language=English)** (low [platelet](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45840&version=Patient&language=English) levels). It is used in:
- Adults with [chronic](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45641&version=Patient&language=English) [immune thrombocytopenia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=793904&version=Patient&language=English) (ITP), a condition in which platelets are destroyed by the [immune system](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46356&version=Patient&language=English).
- Children aged 1 year and older who have had ITP for at least 6 months.

Romiplostim is used in certain patients with ITP who have not gotten better with other treatment.
