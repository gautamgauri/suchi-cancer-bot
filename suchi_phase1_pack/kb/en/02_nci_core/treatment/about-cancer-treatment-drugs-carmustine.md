## Use in Cancer

Carmustine
is approved
to be used alone or with other [drugs](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=348921&version=Patient&language=English) to treat:

- **[Brain tumors](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=387264&version=Patient&language=English)** (certain types).
- **[Hodgkin lymphoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=270800&version=Patient&language=English).** It is used with other drugs in patients whose disease has not gotten better with other treatment or has [recurred](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46556&version=Patient&language=English) (come back).
- **[Multiple myeloma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45793&version=Patient&language=English).** It is used with [prednisone](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45492&version=Patient&language=English).
- **[Non-Hodgkin lymphoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45148&version=Patient&language=English).** It is used with other drugs in patients whose disease has not gotten better with other treatment or has recurred (come back).

Carmustine
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
[cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).

Carmustine is also available in a different form called [carmustine implant](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=762307&version=Patient&language=English). For more information, see the Drug Information Summary for [Carmustine Implant](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/carmustineimplant).
