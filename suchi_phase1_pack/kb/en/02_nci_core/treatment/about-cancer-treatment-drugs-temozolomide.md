## Use in Cancer

Temozolomide is approved to treat the following types of brain cancer in adults:

- **[Anaplastic](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45591&version=Patient&language=English) [astrocytoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45602&version=Patient&language=English).** It is used:
- As [adjuvant treatment](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45587&version=Patient&language=English) in patients whose cancer is newly diagnosed.
- In patients whose cancer did not respond to therapy.
- **[Glioblastoma multiforme](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45699&version=Patient&language=English)** (GBM). It is used with [radiation therapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44295&version=Patient&language=English) in patients with newly-diagnosed cancer and then alone as [maintenance therapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45768&version=Patient&language=English).

Temozolomide is also being studied in the treatment of other types of cancer.
