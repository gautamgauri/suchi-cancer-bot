## Use in Cancer

Luspatercept-aamt
is approved to treat:

- **[Anemia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45360&version=Patient&language=English)** caused by certain types of [myelodysplastic syndromes](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45266&version=Patient&language=English) or myelodysplastic/[myeloproliferative neoplasms](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45210&version=Patient&language=English). It is used in adults who need regular [red blood cell](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46124&version=Patient&language=English) [transfusions](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46001&version=Patient&language=English) and whose anemia did not respond to or cannot be treated with an [erythropoiesis-stimulating agent](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=747357&version=Patient&language=English).

Luspatercept-aamt
is also being studied in the treatment of other conditions.
