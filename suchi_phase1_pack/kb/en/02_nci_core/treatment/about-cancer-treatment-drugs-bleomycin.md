# Bleomycin Sulfate - NCI

# Bleomycin Sulfate

Placeholder slot

(blee-oh-MY-sin SUL-fayt)

Bleomycin sulfate is an antibiotic that is used as a chemotherapy drug. It stops or slows the growth of cancer cells and other rapidly growing cells by damaging their DNA.

FDA Approved

Yes

[FDA label information for this drug is available at DailyMed.](https://dailymed.nlm.nih.gov/dailymed/search.cfm?labeltype=all&query=bleomycin&pagesize=20&page=1&audience=consumer)

## Use in Cancer

Bleomycin sulfate is approved to be used alone or with other [drugs](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=348921&version=Patient&language=English) as [palliative treatment](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45815&version=Patient&language=English) of:

- **[Hodgkin lymphoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=270800&version=Patient&language=English).**
- **[Non-Hodgkin lymphoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45148&version=Patient&language=English)** (NHL).
- **[Squamous cell carcinoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46595&version=Patient&language=English) of the [penis](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=446430&version=Patient&language=English).**
- **Squamous cell carcinoma of the [cervix](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46133&version=Patient&language=English).**
- **[Squamous cell carcinoma of the head and neck](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=597171&version=Patient&language=English)** (SCCHN).
- **Squamous cell carcinoma of the [vulva](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44974&version=Patient&language=English).**
- **[Testicular cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=445090&version=Patient&language=English).**

Bleomycin sulfate is also approved to treat **[malignant pleural effusion](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=524207&version=Patient&language=English)** and keep it from [recurring](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46556&version=Patient&language=English) (coming back).

Bleomycin sulfate is also being studied in the treatment of other types of cancer.
