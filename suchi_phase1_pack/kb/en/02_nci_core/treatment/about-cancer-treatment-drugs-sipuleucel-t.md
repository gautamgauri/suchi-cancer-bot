## Use in Cancer

Sipuleucel-T
is approved to treat:

- **[Prostate cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=445079&version=Patient&language=English)** that has [metastasized](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46283&version=Patient&language=English) (spread to other parts of the body). It is used in men who have few or no [symptoms](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45022&version=Patient&language=English) and whose [cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English) is [hormone-refractory](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=686077&version=Patient&language=English) (does not [respond](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44085&version=Patient&language=English) to [hormone treatment](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44525&version=Patient&language=English)).
