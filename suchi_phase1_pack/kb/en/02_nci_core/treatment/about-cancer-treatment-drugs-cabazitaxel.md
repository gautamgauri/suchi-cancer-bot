## Use in Cancer

Cabazitaxel is approved to be used with [prednisone](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45492&version=Patient&language=English) to treat:

- **[Prostate cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=445079&version=Patient&language=English)** that has [metastasized](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46283&version=Patient&language=English) (spread to other parts of the body) in men whose [cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English) is [castrate resistant](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=753525&version=Patient&language=English) (has not responded to treatments that lower [testosterone](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45581&version=Patient&language=English) levels) and who have already been treated with [chemotherapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45214&version=Patient&language=English) that included [docetaxel](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45382&version=Patient&language=English).

Cabazitaxel
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
cancer.
