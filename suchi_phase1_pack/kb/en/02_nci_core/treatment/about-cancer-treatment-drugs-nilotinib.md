## Use in Cancer

Nilotinib is approved to treat:

- **[Chronic myelogenous leukemia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44901&version=Patient&language=English)** (CML) that is [Philadelphia chromosome](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44179&version=Patient&language=English) [positive](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44710&version=Patient&language=English). It is used:
- In adults and children aged 1 year or older with newly [diagnosed](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46450&version=Patient&language=English) [chronic phase CML](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45160&version=Patient&language=English).
- In adults with chronic phase or [accelerated phase CML](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46212&version=Patient&language=English) that cannot be treated with or that did not [respond](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44085&version=Patient&language=English) to treatment that included [imatinib mesylate](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44893&version=Patient&language=English).
- In children aged 1 year or older with chronic phase CML that cannot be treated with or that did not respond to [tyrosine kinase inhibitor](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44833&version=Patient&language=English) [therapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44737&version=Patient&language=English).

Nilotinib is also being studied in the treatment of other types of cancer.
