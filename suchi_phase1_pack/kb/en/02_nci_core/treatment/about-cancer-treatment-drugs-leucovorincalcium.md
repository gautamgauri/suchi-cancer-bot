# Leucovorin Calcium - NCI

# Leucovorin Calcium

Placeholder slot

(LOO-koh-VOR-in KAL-see-um)

Leucovorin calcium is a type of drug called a folic acid analog, which means it is similar to the vitamin folic acid. It is used in combination with certain chemotherapy drugs to enhance their ability to kill cancer cells (for example, fluorouracil) or to lessen their harmful side effects (for example, methotrexate).

## Use in Cancer

Leucovorin calcium is approved to be used alone or with other drugs to treat:

- **[Colorectal cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=444983&version=Patient&language=English).** It is used with [fluorouracil](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46090&version=Patient&language=English) as [palliative treatment](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45815&version=Patient&language=English) in patients with [advanced](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=478743&version=Patient&language=English) disease.
- **[Anemia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45360&version=Patient&language=English).** It is used to treat megaloblastic anemia that occurs when the body does not get enough of a vitamin called [folic acid](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45236&version=Patient&language=English). It is used by patients who cannot take the vitamin by mouth.

Leucovorin calcium is also used to prevent and treat the toxic [effects](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46580&version=Patient&language=English) of [high-dose](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=346522&version=Patient&language=English) [methotrexate](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45393&version=Patient&language=English) when used to treat [osteosarcoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45395&version=Patient&language=English) and other types of cancer. It is also used to treat [overdoses](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=450114&version=Patient&language=English) of methotrexate or other folic acid [antagonists](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=350250&version=Patient&language=English).

The drug is also being studied in the treatment of other conditions and types of cancer.
