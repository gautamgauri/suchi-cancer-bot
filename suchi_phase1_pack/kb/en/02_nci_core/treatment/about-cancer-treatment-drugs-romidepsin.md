## Use in Cancer

Romidepsin is approved to treat:

- **[Cutaneous T-cell lymphoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46771&version=Patient&language=English)** in adults who have already been treated with at least one other type of [systemic therapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45922&version=Patient&language=English).

Romidepsin is also being studied in the treatment of other types of [cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
