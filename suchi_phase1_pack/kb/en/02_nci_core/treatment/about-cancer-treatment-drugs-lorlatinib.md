## Use in Cancer

Lorlatinib is approved to treat adults with:

- **[Non-small cell lung cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45323&version=Patient&language=English)** that is [ALK positive](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=796880&version=Patient&language=English) and has spread to other parts of the body.

Lorlatinib
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
cancer.
