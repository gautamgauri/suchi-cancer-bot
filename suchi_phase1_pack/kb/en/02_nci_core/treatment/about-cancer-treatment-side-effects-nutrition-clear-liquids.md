# Clear Liquids

If you have [appetite loss](/about-cancer/treatment/side-effects/appetite-loss), [constipation](/about-cancer/treatment/side-effects/constipation), [diarrhea](/about-cancer/treatment/side-effects/diarrhea), or [vomiting](/about-cancer/treatment/side-effects/nausea-vomiting), your doctor or dietitian may suggest that you have plenty of clear liquids. Get ideas on what to drink from this list of clear liquids.

## **Soups**

- bouillon
- clear, fat-free broth

## **Drinks**

- clear apple juice
- clear carbonated beverages
- fruit-flavored drinks
- fruit punch
- sports drinks
- water
- weak, caffeine-free tea

## **Sweets**

- fruit ices made without fruit pieces or milk
- gelatin (Jell-O)
- honey
- jelly
- popsicles

## **Nutritional supplements**

- clear nutrition supplements, such as Boost Breeze and Ensure Clear
