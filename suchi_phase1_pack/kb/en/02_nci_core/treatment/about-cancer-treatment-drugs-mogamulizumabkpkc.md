## Use in Cancer

Mogamulizumab-kpkc
is approved to treat:

- **[Mycosis fungoides](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45794&version=Patient&language=English)** or **[Sezary syndrome](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45878&version=Patient&language=English)** (types of [cutaneous T-cell lymphoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46771&version=Patient&language=English)). It is used to treat disease that has [relapsed](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45866&version=Patient&language=English) (come back) or is [refractory](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=350245&version=Patient&language=English) (does not [respond](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44085&version=Patient&language=English) to treatment) in adult patients who have received at least one other type of [systemic therapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45922&version=Patient&language=English).

Mogamulizumab-kpkc
is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
[cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
