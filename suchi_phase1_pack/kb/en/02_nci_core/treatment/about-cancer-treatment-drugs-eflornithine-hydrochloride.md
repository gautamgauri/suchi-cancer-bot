## Use in Cancer

Eflornithine hydrochloride
is approved to treat:

- **[Neuroblastoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45418&version=Patient&language=English)** that is [high risk](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45079&version=Patient&language=English). It is used in adults and children who had a [partial](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45819&version=Patient&language=English) or [complete response](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45652&version=Patient&language=English) to [combination therapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=688810&version=Patient&language=English) that included anti-GD2 [immunotherapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45729&version=Patient&language=English).

Eflornithine hydrochloride
is also being studied in the treatment of other conditions and types of
cancer.
