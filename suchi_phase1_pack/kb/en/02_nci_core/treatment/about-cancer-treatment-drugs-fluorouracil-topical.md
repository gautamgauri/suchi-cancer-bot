## Use in Cancer

Topical fluorouracil
is approved to treat:

- **[Actinic keratosis](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45133&version=Patient&language=English).** This use is approved for the Carac, Efudex, Fluoroplex, and Tolak brands of topical fluorouracil.

The Efudex brand of topical fluorouracil is also used to treat **[basal cell carcinoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46515&version=Patient&language=English)** that is [superficial](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44305&version=Patient&language=English). It is used in patients whose [tumors](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46634&version=Patient&language=English) cannot be treated with [conventional therapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44198&version=Patient&language=English).

Fluorouracil is also called 5-FU.

Fluorouracil is also available in an [injection](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44678&version=Patient&language=English) form. For more information, see the Drug Information Summary for [Fluorouracil Injection](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/fluorouracil).
