# FOLFIRI-CETUXIMAB

Placeholder slot

(... seh-TUK-sih-mab )

This combination contains the chemotherapy drugs leucovorin calcium (folinic acid), fluorouracil, and irinotecan hydrochloride and the targeted therapy drug cetuximab. Combinations usually work better than single drugs because
different drugs kill
cancer
cells in different ways.

Drugs in the FOLFIRI-CETUXIMAB combination:

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **FOL** | = [Leucovorin Calcium](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/leucovorincalcium) (Folinic Acid) |
| **F** | = [Fluorouracil](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/fluorouracil) |
| **IRI** | = [Irinotecan Hydrochloride](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/irinotecanhydrochloride) |
|  | + [Cetuximab](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/cetuximab) |

Each of the drugs in this combination is approved by the
[Food and Drug
Administration (FDA)](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=454785&version=Patient&language=English)
to treat cancer or conditions related to cancer.

## Use in Cancer

FOLFIRI-CETUXIMAB
is used to treat:

- **[Colorectal cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=444983&version=Patient&language=English)**. It is used in patients whose cancer has a certain [mutation](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46063&version=Patient&language=English) of the *[KRAS](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=652256&version=Patient&language=English)* [gene](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45693&version=Patient&language=English) and has [metastasized](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46283&version=Patient&language=English) (spread to other parts of the body).

This combination may also be used with other drugs or
treatments or to treat other types of cancer.
