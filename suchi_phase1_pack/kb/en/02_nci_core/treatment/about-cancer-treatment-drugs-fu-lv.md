## Use in Cancer

FU-LV is used to treat:

- **[Colorectal cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=444983&version=Patient&language=English).**
- **[Esophageal cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=444989&version=Patient&language=English).**
- **[Gastric cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=454513&version=Patient&language=English).**

This combination may also be used with other drugs or treatments or to treat other types of cancer.
