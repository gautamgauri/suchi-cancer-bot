# CHLORAMBUCIL-PREDNISONE - NCI

# CHLORAMBUCIL-PREDNISONE

Placeholder slot

(klor-AM-byoo-sil-PRED-nih-sone)

This combination contains the chemotherapy drug chlorambucil and the steroid hormone prednisone. Combinations usually work better than single drugs because
different drugs kill
cancer
cells in different ways.

Drugs in the CHLORAMBUCIL-PREDNISONE combination:

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
|  | [Chlorambucil](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/chlorambucil) |
|  | [Prednisone](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/prednisone) |

Each of the
drugs
in this
combination
is approved by the
[Food and Drug
Administration (FDA)](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=454785&version=Patient&language=English)
to treat
cancer
or conditions related to cancer.

## Use in Cancer

CHLORAMBUCIL-PREDNISONE is used to treat:

- **[Chronic lymphocytic leukemia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44846&version=Patient&language=English)** (CLL).

This combination may also be used with other drugs or treatments or to treat other types of cancer.
