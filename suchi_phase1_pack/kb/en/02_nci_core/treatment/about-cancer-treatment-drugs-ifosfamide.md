## Use in Cancer

Ifosfamide
is approved to be used with other [drugs](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=348921&version=Patient&language=English) to treat:

- **[Testicular](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46611&version=Patient&language=English) [germ cell tumors](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45695&version=Patient&language=English)** that are [malignant](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45772&version=Patient&language=English). It is used in patients who have already been treated with two other types of [chemotherapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45214&version=Patient&language=English).

Ifosfamide is also being studied in the treatment of other types of
[cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
