## Use in Cancer

Rolapitant hydrochloride
is approved to be used with other [drugs](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=348921&version=Patient&language=English) to [prevent](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=439419&version=Patient&language=English):

- **[Nausea](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=390302&version=Patient&language=English)** and **[vomiting](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=390324&version=Patient&language=English)** caused by [chemotherapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45214&version=Patient&language=English). It is used in adults.
