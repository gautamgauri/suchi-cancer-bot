## Use in Cancer

Defibrotide sodium
is approved to treat:

- **[Hepatic veno-occlusive disease](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44806&version=Patient&language=English)** (also known as sinusoidal obstruction syndrome) in adults and children who also have [kidney](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46325&version=Patient&language=English) or [lung](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=270740&version=Patient&language=English) problems after receiving a [stem cell transplant](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46695&version=Patient&language=English).
