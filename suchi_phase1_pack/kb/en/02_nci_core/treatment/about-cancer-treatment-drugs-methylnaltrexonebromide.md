## Use in Cancer

Methylnaltrexone bromide
is approved to treat:

- **[Constipation](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=407757&version=Patient&language=English)** caused by the use of [opioids](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45054&version=Patient&language=English). It is used in adults with pain caused by [cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English) or treatment for cancer or by other [advanced disease](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=478743&version=Patient&language=English).
