# COPDAC - NCI

# COPDAC

This combination contains the chemotherapy drugs cyclophosphamide, vincristine sulfate (Oncovin), and dacarbazine and the steroid hormone prednisone. Combinations usually work better than single drugs because
different drugs kill
cancer
cells in different ways.

Drugs in the COPDAC combination:

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **C** | = [Cyclophosphamide](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/cyclophosphamide) |
| **O** | = [Vincristine Sulfate](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/vincristinesulfate) (Oncovin) |
| **P** | = [Prednisone](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/prednisone) |
| **DAC** | = [Dacarbazine](/about-cancer/treatment/drugs/dacarbazine) |

Each of the drugs in this combination is approved by the
[Food and Drug
Administration (FDA)](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=454785&version=Patient&language=English)
to treat cancer or conditions related to cancer.

## Use in Cancer

COPDAC
is used to treat:

- **[Hodgkin lymphoma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=270800&version=Patient&language=English)** in children. It is used to treat certain male patients and is often used with [radiation therapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=44971&version=Patient&language=English).

This combination may also be used with other drugs or
treatments or to treat other types of cancer.
