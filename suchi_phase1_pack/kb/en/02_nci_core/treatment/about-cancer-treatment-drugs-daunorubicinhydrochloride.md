## Use in Cancer

Daunorubicin hydrochloride is approved to be used with other [drugs](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=348921&version=Patient&language=English) as [remission induction therapy](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46560&version=Patient&language=English) to treat:

- **[Acute lymphoblastic leukemia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45586&version=Patient&language=English)** in adults and children.
- **[Acute myeloid leukemia](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46347&version=Patient&language=English)** in adults.

It is also being studied in the treatment of other types of [cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
