## Use in Cancer

Glucarpidase
is approved
to treat:

- **[Toxic](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=43986&version=Patient&language=English) [blood](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=270735&version=Patient&language=English) levels of the [anticancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=639945&version=Patient&language=English) [drug](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=348921&version=Patient&language=English) [methotrexate](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45393&version=Patient&language=English).** Glucarpidase is used to remove methotrexate from the body in adults and children whose [kidneys](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46325&version=Patient&language=English) are not working as they should.
