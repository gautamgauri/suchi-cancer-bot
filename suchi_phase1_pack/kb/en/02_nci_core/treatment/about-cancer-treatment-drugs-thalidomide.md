## Use in Cancer

Thalidomide is approved to be used with [dexamethasone](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45262&version=Patient&language=English) to treat:

- **[Multiple myeloma](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45793&version=Patient&language=English)** in patients who have just been [diagnosed](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=46450&version=Patient&language=English) with the disease.

Thalidomide is only available as part of a special program called Thalomid REMS ([Risk Evaluation and Mitigation Strategies](http://www.thalomidrems.com/)).

Thalidomide is also being studied in the treatment of other types of [cancer](/Common/PopUps/popDefinition.aspx?id=45333&version=Patient&language=English).
