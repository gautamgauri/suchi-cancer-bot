# PDQ® Cancer Genetics Editorial Board

This Editorial Board consists of experts in the fields of genetics, clinical oncology, epidemiology, psychology, and the social sciences. This editorial board meets three times a year, and the primary purpose is to develop and maintain PDQ cancer information summaries pertaining to the field of cancer genetics.
