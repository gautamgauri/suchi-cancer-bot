# PDQÂ® Screening and Prevention Editorial Board

Members of this Board represent the fields of oncology, cancer prevention, cancer screening, hematology, radiology, urology, statistics, epidemiology, and economics. The Screening and Prevention Editorial Board currently meets six times a year to write and update information on cancer screening (early detection) and prevention.
