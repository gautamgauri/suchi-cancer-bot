# PDQ® Cancer Genetics Summaries - NCI

# PDQ® Cancer Information Summaries: Genetics

[Cancer Genetics Overview (PDQ®) – health professional](/publications/pdq/information-summaries/genetics/overview-hp-pdq): Includes information about genetic counseling, familial cancer susceptibility syndromes, clinical sequencing, and genetics resources.

[Cancer Genetics Risk Assessment and Counseling (PDQ®) – health professional](/publications/pdq/information-summaries/genetics/risk-assessment-hp-pdq): Includes the components of cancer genetics risk assessment and counseling, genetic testing considerations, psychosocial assessments, and the ethical, legal, and social implications of genetic testing.

[Genetics of Breast and Gynecologic Cancers (PDQ®) – health professional](/types/breast/hp/breast-ovarian-genetics-pdq): Includes *BRCA1*/*BRCA2*, Lynch syndrome, *PALB2*, Li-Fraumeni syndrome, hereditary diffuse gastric cancer, moderate risk genes, and other genes/syndromes.

[Genetics of Colorectal Cancer (PDQ®) – health professional](/types/colorectal/hp/colorectal-genetics-pdq): Includes Lynch syndrome, familial adenomatous polyposis, MUTYH-associated polyposis, and other genes/syndromes.

[Genetics of Endocrine and Neuroendocrine Neoplasias (PDQ®) – health professional](/types/thyroid/hp/medullary-thyroid-genetics-pdq): Includes multiple endocrine neoplasia type 1 (MEN1), MEN2, MEN4, familial pheochromocytoma and paraganglioma syndrome, Carney-Stratakis syndrome, and familial nonmedullary thyroid cancer.

[Genetics of Gastric Cancer (PDQ®) – health professional](/types/stomach/hp/gastric-genetics-pdq): Includes hereditary diffuse gastric cancer (*CDH1* and *CTNNA1* genes) and other syndromes.

[Genetics of Hereditary Hematologic Malignancies](/publications/pdq/information-summaries/genetics/hereditary-hematologic-malignancies): Includes several genes and hereditary syndromes associated with hematologic malignancies.

[Genetics of Renal Cell Carcinoma (Kidney Cancer) (PDQ®) – health professional](/types/kidney/hp/renal-cell-carcinoma-genetics), [Genetics of Kidney Cancer (PDQ®) – patient version](/types/kidney/patient/kidney-genetics-pdq): Includes Von-Hippel Lindau Disease, Hereditary Leiomyomatosis and Renal Cell Cancer, Hereditary Papillary Renal Carcinoma, and Birt-Hogg-Dubé Syndrome.

[Genetics of Prostate Cancer (PDQ®) – health professional](/types/prostate/hp/prostate-genetics-pdq): Includes *BRCA1*/*BRCA2*, *HOXB13*, Lynch syndrome, *ATM* and *CHEK2*.

[Genetics of Skin Cancer (PDQ®) – health professional](/types/skin/hp/skin-genetics-pdq): Includes several genes and hereditary syndromes associated with:

- Basal cell carcinoma, such as basal cell nevus syndrome and xeroderma pigmentosum
- Squamous cell carcinoma, such as epidermolysis bullosa and Fanconi anemia
- Melanoma, such as *CDKN2A*, *MITF*, and *BAP1*
