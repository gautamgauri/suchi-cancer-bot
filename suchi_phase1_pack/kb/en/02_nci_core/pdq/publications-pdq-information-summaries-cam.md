# PDQ® Cancer Information Summaries: Integrative, Alternative, and Complementary Therapies

This page lists cancer information summaries maintained by the PDQ Integrative, Alternative, and Complementary Therapies Editorial Board.

##

Summaries Being Regularly Updated

These summaries are reviewed and updated to reflect findings from recent research.

The PDQ Integrative, Alternative, and Complementary Therapies summaries are carefully developed, thorough reviews on controversial topics which often are not addressed in medical education but are of particular interest to, and often used by, many patients.

Jeffrey D. White, MD - PDQ Integrative, Alternative, and Complementary Therapies Editorial Board Member - National Cancer Institute
