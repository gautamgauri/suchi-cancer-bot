## Red flags (seek urgent care now)
- Severe shortness of breath or chest pain
- Fainting/confusion
- Uncontrolled bleeding or vomiting blood
- High fever with severe weakness (especially during chemotherapy)

## What to do now
Go to the nearest emergency department or call local emergency services.
