## Before diagnosis

When you have concerns or symptoms:

- What are the possible causes of my symptoms?
- What tests are needed and how soon should they be done?
- What do these tests involve?
- Are there any risks or side effects of these tests?
- How long will it take to get results?

## After diagnosis

If you receive a cancer diagnosis:

- What is the specific type and stage of cancer (if known)?
- What does the stage mean for my situation?
- What are all the treatment options available?
- What are the goals of each treatment option?
- What are the expected benefits and risks of each treatment?
- What are the side effects I should expect?
- How will treatment affect my daily life?
- Are there clinical trials I might be eligible for?

## During treatment

While undergoing treatment:

- What warning signs need urgent care?
- How should I manage side effects?
- When should I call my care team?
- What should I do if I miss a dose?
- Are there activities I should avoid?
- What dietary changes should I make?
- How will we know if treatment is working?

## General tips

- Write down your questions before appointments
- Bring someone with you to take notes
- Don't hesitate to ask for clarification
- Get a second opinion if you feel uncertain
- Keep copies of your medical records
